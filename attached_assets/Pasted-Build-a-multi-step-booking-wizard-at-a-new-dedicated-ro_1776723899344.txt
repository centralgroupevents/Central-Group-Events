Build a multi-step booking wizard at a new dedicated route /book. This completely replaces the existing "Promote Your Event" booking form section on the homepage — remove that section from the homepage and replace it with a simple centered CTA that says "Ready to promote your event?" with a "Get Started" button that links to /book.

---

ROUTE & PAGE:
- Create a new page at /book
- Add /book to the React router
- Dark themed, matching the rest of the site
- CGE logo at the top, centered
- Page title: "Let's Promote Your Event"
- Subtitle: "Answer a few quick questions and we'll get you set up."

---

WIZARD STRUCTURE:
The wizard has 6 steps total. Show a progress bar at the top with clickable step indicators labeled:
1. Package
2. Event Details
3. Contact Info
4. Logistics
5. Terms
6. Payment

Users can click completed steps to go back. Back/Next buttons at the bottom of each step. "Next" button is disabled until required fields on the current step are filled.

---

STEP 1 — "Which package are you going with?"
Show the three packages as selectable cards side by side:

Starter — $70
- Social Media Page Post (IG, TikTok, FB)
- Instagram & FB Story Post
- Email Blast
- Newsletter Listing

Growth — $150 (show "Most Popular" badge)
- Everything in Starter
- Premium Newsletter Placement
- Event Calendar Feature
- $50 Ad Credit
- SMS Marketing
- Consultation

Custom — $300+
- Everything in Growth
- Influencer Reach
- $100 Ad Credit

Clicking a card selects it (highlighted purple border). Must select one to proceed.

---

STEP 2 — "Tell us about your event" (2 fields)
- Event Name (text input, required)
- Event Type (select dropdown: Brunch, Concert, Dance Class, DJ Set, Festival, Happy Hour, Live Music, Party, Special Event, Other)
- If "Other" selected, show a text input: "Describe your event type"

---

STEP 3 — "When and where?" (2 fields per screen)
Screen A:
- Event Date (date picker, required)
- Event Time (time input, required)

Screen B (next within same step):
- Venue Name (text input, required)
- City (text input, required)
- Region (select: North NJ / Central NJ / South NJ, required)

Note: Step 3 has two sub-screens. The step indicator stays on Step 3 but the content changes. Show "3a" and "3b" progress within the step.

---

STEP 4 — "How can we reach you?" (2 fields)
Screen A:
- Contact Name (text input, required)
- Email Address (email input, required, validated)

Screen B:
- Phone Number (tel input, required for Growth and Custom, optional for Starter — label it accordingly)
- Instagram Handle (text input, required, @ prefix hint)

---

STEP 5 — "Almost there — please review our terms"
- Show the full Terms & Conditions content (same content as the modal from the booking form)
- Scrollable container, max height 400px
- Checkbox at the bottom: "I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions"
- Cannot proceed without checking the box

---

STEP 6 — "You're almost in! Here's how to complete your booking"
Show a confirmation summary card on the left:
- Selected package + price
- Event Name
- Event Date + Time
- Venue + City + Region
- Contact Name + Email

On the right side:
- Heading: "Complete Your Payment"
- Subtext: "Send your payment via CashApp to lock in your slot. Once received, our team will reach out within 24 hours to confirm your promotion."
- A large green "Pay Now" button linking to https://cash.app/$centralgroupevents in a new tab
- Payment amount shown clearly based on selected package ($70, $150, or $300+)
- Below the button, instructions in muted text:
  1. Click "Pay Now" to open CashApp
  2. Send [amount] to $centralgroupevents
  3. Include your event name in the payment note
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours
- A purple "Book a Strategy Call" button below the instructions linking to https://calendly.com/centralgroupevents/30min in a new tab
- Subtext under Calendly button: "Optional but recommended — especially for Growth and Custom packages"

---

BACKEND:
- On reaching Step 6, automatically save the submission to the database (same bookings table as before) with all collected fields plus the selected package/mode
- Send the confirmation email to the user's email (same email template as previously built — event details + social links + 24hr response promise)
- Send notification email to centralgroupevents@gmail.com with full submission details
- This should happen when the user reaches Step 6, not when they click Pay Now

VALIDATION:
- Each step validates its own required fields before allowing Next
- Show inline red error messages under each field that fails
- Required fields vary by package:
  - Phone is required for Growth and Custom, optional for Starter
  - All other required fields apply to all packages
